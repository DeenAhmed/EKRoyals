About Us

We are a community run charity that focus on helping young people learn to play, coach and referee the game of Basketball.
We run many different training sessions for all high school ages.

We currently compete at local level across several age groups. As the team grows we are rising through the leagues.
This is due to not only our fantatsic players but our Senior Head Coaches, who have both played professionally in Scotland and Britian.

Are you competitive, driven and have a passion for basketball!
